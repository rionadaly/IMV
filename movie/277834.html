Diversity

Disney succeeds at both entertaining and educating with its focus on Moana's self-reliance, leadership, and cultural ties. The intergenerational message of "Moana" is an invitation to learn more about the richness of Pacific Island traditions and to consider the value of maintaining indigenous knowledge in the face of rapid global change.
