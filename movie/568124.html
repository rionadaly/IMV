Diversity

The movie showcases the beauty of heritage with a setting rooted in Colombian culture, from music to traditions. By portraying characters with various body types, personalities, and abilities, "Encanto" sends a powerful message that everyone's differences are what makes them extraordinary. The film's plot highlights the value of diversity celebration, self-acceptance, and tolerance.
