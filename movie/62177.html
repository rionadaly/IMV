Diversity

Merida's bold and independent nature in "Brave" challenges traditional princess stereotypes and emphasises the importance of individuality and self-discovery. The strong-willed young woman with fiery red hair, who lives in the picturesque Scottish Highlands, defies the expectations associated with typical princess roles and inspires women worldwide. The film encourages young viewers, especially girls, to go their own way and pursue their goals without being restricted by societal expectations.
